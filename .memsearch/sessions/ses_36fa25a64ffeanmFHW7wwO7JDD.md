# Journey: Daily Planning

**Actor:** Team Member  
**Goal:** Start day with clear priorities

## Steps

1. Review inbox: scan calendar, messages, and open tasks for new or urgent items.  
2. Prioritize: pick the top items, estimate effort, and order by impact.  
3. Commit to today: select up to three items to finish or make measurable progress on.

## Success

Team Member knows their top 3 priorities within 5 minutes and can state the single most important task for the day.
